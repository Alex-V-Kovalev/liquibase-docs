Maven rollbackOneChangeSet

Full name:

org.liquibase:liquibase-maven-plugin:3.10.1:rollbackOneChangeSet

Description:

Reverts (rolls back) one non-sequential made during a previous change to your database. It is only available for users.

Attributes:

  • Requires a Maven 2.0 project to be executed.
  • Requires dependency resolution of artifacts in scope: test.
