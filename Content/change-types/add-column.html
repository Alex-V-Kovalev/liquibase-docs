addColumn

The addColumn adds a new column to an existing table.

Uses

You can typically use the addColumn when you want to add a new column and deploy it to the table in your database.

Running the addColumn

To create a column for your table, follow these steps:

Step 1: Add the addColumn to your with the needed s as it is shown in the examples.

Step 2: Deploy your by running the update command.

liquibase update

Now, you should see a new column.
