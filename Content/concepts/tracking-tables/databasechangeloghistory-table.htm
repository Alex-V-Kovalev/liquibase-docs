uses the (DBCLH) table to record a history of the [%=General.databasechangelog%] table.

The (DBCL) table tracks all deployments you make to your database, but if you roll back a change, that row is removed from the table. However, the DBCLH tracks every change you make to your DBCL, including deployments, rollbacks, and changelog syncs.

The DBCLH table is designed to provide users with an accessible way to track changes made to the DBCL table. It is not designed to satisfy security compliance audits.
