Certified Databases

The following table shows the list of databases that are officially certified (deeply and formally tested) and databases that are supported primarily by community efforts. If you use , customer support representatives will help you to gain the maximum advantage of it; however, some technical aspects are limited either in testing or capability on certain platforms.

For example, some platforms offer only limited functionality for Stored Logic exports for the snapshot, generateChangelog, and diffChangelog commands.
