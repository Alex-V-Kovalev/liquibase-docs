Activation Notice through Email

Your access is set up when your company purchases Liquibase Enterprise.

Documentation

Documentation for some of our products is maintained on a Confluence site, https://datical-cs.atlassian.net/

  • If you need to access a non-public confluence space, you will get an email from Customer Support with information about how to log in. The main documentation spaces are now public and no longer need login credentials.

The following spaces are starting points for Liquibase Enterprise and DMC documentation:

  • Product Documentation - The main documentation page for current versions of Liquibase Enterprise (public)
  • Deployment Monitoring Console (DMC) - The main documentation page for DMC (public)
  • Liquibase Enterprise Knowledge Base - Explore additional topics regarding our Liquibase Enterprise and DMC products (public)

Activating the Account for Software Downloads

Software downloads of Liquibase Enterprise and DMC are maintained on software.datical.com.

  • Customers will get an email from notifications@thruinc.net, subject User Activation Notification (software.datical.com)
  • If you did not receive a registration email for software.datical.com and need to download Liquibase Enterprise or DMC please contact our Technical Support team to request access.

Support emails you an invitation to set up and activate the account.

  1. Click thelink in the email.

  2. Enter and confirm a password, then click SUBMIT.

    Please note the password guidelines on this page.



  3. When the password meets the guidelines, your account is activated. Click the here link to log in and access the site.

Accessing the Site

  1. In a browser, go tohttps://software.datical.com. Log in.



  2. Open FOLDERS, then COMMON.



  3. There are folders for Datical software. The key folders are these:
    • Datical_DB_Software - downloads for the Liquibase Enterprise and client.
    • Datical_Documentation - PDF files of Datical Documentation exported from the Confluence site: Datical Product Documentation.
    • Datical_DMC_Software - downloads for DMC



  4. If you are ready to download software, go to Downloading the Liquibase Enterprise Installer.
    • Otherwise, look around. Log out when you are done.
