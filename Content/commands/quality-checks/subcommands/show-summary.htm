show-summary

The show-summary sub-command is an update-family command argument that produces a summary list or a verbose summary list of any changesets skipped and why they were skipped.

Uses

--show-summary=summary produces a list of what changesets were skipped and --show-summary=verbose adds the reason it was skipped per changeset in a table.

Syntax

Run the command specifying your values:

liquibse update --show-summary=verbose
liquibase updatecount 1 --show-summary=summary
