Logging in to DMC on Datical Service

Deployment Management Console (DMC), is the user interface for Datical Service:

Logging In

To log in:

  1. Open a browser

  2. Enter the URL where Datical Service is installed. Get the path from the administrator who installed Datical Service. 

    https://<path>

    Note

    If Datical Service has just been started, you may see a "Datical Service is starting up" page.  Your browser should refresh when it is available. 

  3. Log in. What you can see depends on the roles assigned to you. 
    • The initial system credentials are for user support@datical.com, password datical. You must change the password the first time you log in. 
    • See "User Access Requirements" in Prerequisites for Datical Service Installation.

See also Roles and UI Visibility. 

Dashboard

The first page you see is the dashboard of operations statistics. 

The view is constrained to projects where you have Project Viewer access. 

See Viewing the Dashboard. 

My Account

Click My Account to see your role and group assignments. 

Navigating

See Viewing Datical Projects. 

Logging out

Click your user name on the top right, then choose Logout.
